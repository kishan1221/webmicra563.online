Adventure in Kathmandu, Nepal

Jony Bristow

Published on Dec 04 at 10:30 AM

Kathmandu is a spiritual and scenic gem nestled in the Himalayas. My journey started in the bustling streets of Thamel and led me to sacred temples like Swayambhunath and Pashupatinath.

The air is filled with incense, chants, and the calmness of the mountains. I also tried momos (Nepalese dumplings) — delicious! People are kind and warm-hearted.

If you love peace, prayer flags, and panoramic views, Kathmandu will steal your heart. A city of soul and spirit.
